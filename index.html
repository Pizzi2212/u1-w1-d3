Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,

Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweer caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter

FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cranberry That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsmaic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushin on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen Cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium.sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
